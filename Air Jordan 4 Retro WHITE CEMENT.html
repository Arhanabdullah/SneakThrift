Air Jordan 4 Retro "WHITE CEMENT"

The Air Jordan 4 Retro "White Cement" is one of the most iconic sneakers in the Jordan lineup, representing a perfect mix of basketball heritage and timeless style. It features a clean white leather upper with the signature speckled Cement Grey accents on the midsole, wings, and heel tab that give the shoe its distinct identity. Black and Fire Red details on the eyelets and Jumpman logo add sharp contrast, while the visible Air unit in the heel ensures lightweight cushioning and comfort.

Classic, versatile, and full of nostalgia, the Air Jordan 4 Retro "White Cement" continues to stand out as a true symbol of basketball culture and timeless design.
