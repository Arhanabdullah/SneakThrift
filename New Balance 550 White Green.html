New Balance 550 White Green

The New Balance 550 “White Green” is a clean, heritage-inspired sneaker that perfectly captures the spirit of classic basketball style. Originally introduced in the late ’80s as a court shoe, the 550 has made a strong comeback as a modern lifestyle essential, and this White Green colorway embodies that timeless appeal. Featuring a smooth white leather upper with contrasting green accents on the collar, branding, and outsole, the shoe offers a crisp, vintage look that pairs effortlessly with any outfit. The perforated panels enhance breathability, while the retro nylon tongue and classic “N” logo preserve its old-school charm. Underfoot, the rubber cupsole and EVA midsole deliver reliable comfort and durability for all-day wear. Combining minimalist design with nostalgic flair, the New Balance 550 “White Green” stands out as the perfect balance of sporty heritage and modern street style.
